More flexible and convenient operation.

You can find all apps you have right to read in sidebar.

Show both logo and name.

Allow users to control the app sidebar enable or not.

You can control the app sidebar enable or not at the top right-hand corner: Settings -> Preference.

Click on app name, show or hide app sidebar.

If sidebar is not enable, open the first action of the app.

Full screen form view and new skin as same as web_responsive model in apps store.
